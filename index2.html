Our Team

We are a tight-knit group at Students Offering Support. With 4 members out of head office, we are also complemented by a team of interns ranging from IT, Finance, Admin Ops and Events. We take pride in supporting the model and by providing support to our SOS chapters, we strive to deliver access to education for those who need it most!
